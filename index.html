Three steps. Zero effort.

Rapport handles the research, the writing, and the timing. You handle the relationship.

1

Connect your CRM

Authorise HubSpot in two clicks. Rapport reads your active contacts — anyone you've engaged with in the last 12 months. Read-only. Nothing is written back to your CRM.

2

Rapport does the work

Every Sunday night, Rapport scans a 14-day window of national, religious, and cultural holidays across your contacts' countries. Claude AI generates personalised subject lines and bodies — not templates.

3

One click. It's sent.

Monday morning you get a digest with every relevant contact and their pre-written greeting. Click "Open draft" — your email client opens with the message ready. Done in 60 seconds.

Rapport never sends email on your behalf.

Every draft opens in your own email client via a mailto link. You review, edit if you want, and press send. You are always the sender.
