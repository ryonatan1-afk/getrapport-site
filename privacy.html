Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Rapport ("we", "us", or "our") provides a relationship intelligence tool that monitors your CRM contacts for upcoming holidays and generates personalised greeting drafts. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

If you have questions, contact us at hello@getrapport.app.

1. Data we collect

When you connect your HubSpot account, Rapport reads the following fields from your CRM contacts via HubSpot's OAuth API:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Country
  • Company name
  • Last activity date (to determine whether a contact is active)

We also collect your name and email address when you sign up, and we store your weekly digest preferences (delivery time, timezone).

2. How we use your data

We use contact data solely to:

  • Identify upcoming national, religious, and cultural holidays relevant to each contact's country
  • Generate personalised AI greeting drafts using Claude (Anthropic)
  • Deliver your weekly digest email every Monday

We do not use your contact data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than delivering the Rapport service to you.

3. What we never do

  • We never write to your CRM. Our HubSpot integration is read-only.
  • We never send emails to your contacts on your behalf. Every draft is opened in your own email client for you to review and send.
  • We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.
  • We never store more contact data than is needed to generate greetings.

4. Third-party services

Rapport uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • HubSpot — CRM integration via OAuth. Governed by HubSpot's privacy policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI generation of greeting drafts. Contact data passed to Claude is used only for generation and is not stored or used to train models under our API agreement.
  • Google Analytics — Website analytics (marketing site only). No contact data is passed to Google Analytics.

5. Data retention

We retain your contact data for as long as your Rapport account is active. When you disconnect HubSpot or delete your account, your contact data is deleted within 30 days. You can request immediate deletion by emailing hello@getrapport.app.

6. Security

We access your HubSpot data using encrypted OAuth tokens with read-only scope. Data is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not store OAuth tokens in plaintext.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect Rapport from HubSpot at any time via your HubSpot Connected Apps settings
  • Unsubscribe from the weekly digest at any time via the unsubscribe link in any digest email

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@getrapport.app.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We will notify active users by email of any material changes. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at hello@getrapport.app.
