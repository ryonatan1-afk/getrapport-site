About this holiday

What is Lunar New Year?

Lunar New Year, celebrated by over a billion people across China, Korea, Vietnam, and East Asian diaspora communities worldwide, is the most important holiday of the year for hundreds of millions of people. The 15-day celebration begins on the first new moon of the lunisolar calendar and includes family reunions, traditional feasts, red envelopes of money, fireworks, lantern festivals, and visits to temples. Each year is associated with one of twelve zodiac animals, and the occasion is filled with wishes for health, prosperity, and good fortune.
