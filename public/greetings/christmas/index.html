About this holiday

What is Christmas?

Christmas, celebrated on December 25th, commemorates the birth of Jesus Christ and is one of the world's most widely observed holidays — celebrated by Christians and non-Christians alike across nearly every country on earth. The modern Christmas season blends Christian traditions with centuries of folk customs: decorating trees, exchanging gifts, singing carols, and gathering with family for festive meals. For hundreds of millions of people, Christmas represents warmth, generosity, and the spirit of giving — and the close of the year is an ideal moment to express gratitude to the people you work with.
